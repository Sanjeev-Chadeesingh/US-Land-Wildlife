Land+Life

This site is a curation of land and wildlife pictures taken throughout the United States. A few years ago, Jason Schneider decided to attempt highlighting the natural composition all around us. He wants to share this composition with as many people as possible. A digital Canon Rebel-T1i took every photograph.
