Land+Life

This site is a curation of land and wildlife pictures taken throughout the United States. A few years ago, Jason Schneider had found that nature was reaching out to him, in what he felt was an attempt to communicate. The best way he knew to respond was by highlighting the composition that nature provided. His main objective is to share this composition with as many people as possible. A digital Canon Rebel-T1i took every photograph.
